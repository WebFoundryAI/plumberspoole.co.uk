Professional Boiler Repair Services

When your boiler breaks down, you need a qualified engineer who can diagnose and fix the problem quickly. Plumbers Poole provides boiler repair services across Poole and the Dorset coast, with Gas Safe registered engineers experienced in all major boiler brands.

Common Boiler Problems We Fix

  • No heating or hot water
  • Boiler not igniting
  • Leaking or dripping boilers
  • Low pressure issues
  • Strange noises
  • Error codes and faults
  • Thermostat problems
  • Radiators not heating

Annual Boiler Servicing

Regular servicing keeps your boiler safe and efficient. Our annual service includes full inspection, safety checks, and combustion analysis. This maintains warranty validity and prevents unexpected breakdowns.

New Boiler Installations

When repair is not economical, we install new energy-efficient boilers with full manufacturer warranties. We advise on the right boiler for your property and hot water requirements.
