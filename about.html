About Plumbers Poole

Your trusted local plumbing experts

Local Plumbers You Can Trust

Plumbers Poole has been serving the local community for many years. We are local plumbers based in Poole, providing reliable services to homes, businesses, and marine vessels across the Dorset coast.

Coastal Property Expertise

Living and working on the Dorset coast, we understand the unique challenges coastal properties face. Salt air corrosion, weather exposure, and the demands of seaside living all require experienced handling.

Marine Plumbing

With Poole Harbour on our doorstep, we provide marine plumbing services for boats and vessels, understanding the specialist requirements of marine installations.

Our Values

  • Reliability - We arrive when we say we will
  • Honesty - Clear pricing with no hidden charges
  • Quality - Professional workmanship on every job
  • Community - Supporting local residents and businesses

Qualifications

All our plumbers are fully qualified and insured. Our gas engineers are Gas Safe registered.

Service Area

We cover Poole, Bournemouth, Christchurch, Wimborne, Wareham, and throughout Dorset.
