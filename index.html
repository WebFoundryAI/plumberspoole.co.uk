Coastal and Marine Expertise

Poole Harbour is one of the largest natural harbours in the world, home to thousands of vessels and several marinas. We provide marine plumbing services for boats moored in the harbour and local marinas, understanding the specific requirements of vessel plumbing systems. From fresh water systems to heads and holding tanks, we have the experience to maintain and repair marine installations.

The coastal environment affects all properties in the area. Salt air accelerates corrosion of metal components and exposed pipework. We advise on appropriate materials and maintenance schedules to maximise the lifespan of plumbing installations in coastal properties. Our experience means we understand these challenges and can recommend solutions that stand up to the seaside environment.
