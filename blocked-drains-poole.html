Professional Drain Clearance

Blocked drains cause inconvenience and can lead to bigger problems if not addressed promptly. Plumbers Poole provides drain unblocking services across Poole and the Dorset coast, using professional equipment to clear blockages effectively.

Blockages We Clear

  • Blocked sinks - Kitchen and bathroom
  • Blocked toilets - Complete blockages and slow draining
  • Blocked baths and showers - Hair and soap build-up
  • Blocked external drains - Gullies and manholes
  • Blocked soil pipes - Main drainage blockages

Our Methods

  • Plunging and rodding - For accessible blockages
  • High-pressure jetting - For stubborn blockages
  • CCTV inspection - To diagnose complex problems

Properties near Poole Harbour can experience particular drainage challenges. We understand local drainage systems and can address issues specific to coastal properties.
