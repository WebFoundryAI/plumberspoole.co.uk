Professional Bathroom Installations

Transform your bathroom with Plumbers Poole. We provide complete bathroom installation services across Poole and the Dorset coast, from initial design through to the finishing touches. Whether you want a simple suite change or a complete bathroom transformation, our team delivers quality results.

Our Bathroom Services

  • Full bathroom renovations - Complete strip-out and refit
  • En-suite installations - Creating additional bathroom facilities
  • Wet room conversions - Accessible walk-in shower rooms
  • Shower installations - All types of shower systems
  • Bath replacements - Standard to freestanding baths
  • Sanitary ware fitting - Toilets, basins, and more

Coastal Property Bathrooms

Installing bathrooms in coastal properties requires consideration of the environment. We advise on appropriate materials and finishes that resist the effects of salt air and humidity, ensuring your bathroom looks great and lasts for years.

Our Process

  1. Consultation - Discuss your requirements and measure the space
  2. Design - Plan the layout and recommend products
  3. Quote - Receive a detailed, fixed-price quote
  4. Installation - Complete professional fitting
  5. Handover - Final inspection and demonstration

Areas Covered
